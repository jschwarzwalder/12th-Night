An Tir 12th Night 51 Celebration

Long live the King and Queen of AnTir!

The Barony of Wyewood is again proud to host An Tir’s Twelfth Night Coronation.

Join us as we attend King Kjartan and Queen Sha’ya at their final court; rejoice at the coronation of Jarl Styrkarr and Dagrun Stjarna as the new monarchs of this great land.

There will be much to enjoy at this event - the Queen’s Rapier Championship Tournament, auctions, contests, merchants, plus an entire floor of vigils and royalty!

Hosted by the Barony of Wyewood in SeaTac, Washington
January 13-15th, 2017 (A.S. LI)
